About

Welcome to the page where I want to tell you a little about myself.

Film Photography

Since my early teenage years I always been interested in Film Photography, or 35mm pictures. This love came from my brother’s hobby of making extremely “cozy” and cute pictures of his friends on 35mm old camera that he got from our parents. Pictures on 35mm camera give me way more emotions and memories than digital ones, at least the process of making them, when you do not really know what you’ll get at the end of your film.

Make up

I’ve always been interested in painting, but not really in make up. Everything changed from the time I had an opportunity to try myself on the photoshoot, where the photographer asked me to help him with minimalistic make up for models. It became one of the methods for me to express my ideas through pictures, which I still continue to learn.

Style

I love fashion and experiments. I always try to express myself and my mood through clothes that I wear. I do not have any specific preferences and the golden middle in my style, because I like mixing colours and oversize clothes, while at the same time I love minimalistic and very elegant style.
